	Community Radiology Associates (CRA) | Olney Imaging Center is part of CRA’s network of outpatient radiology centers located throughout Carroll, Frederick, Montgomery and Prince George’s counties in Maryland.
</p><p>
	Olney Imaging Center offers a complete range of imaging services including an open MRI/MRA unit to accommodate claustrophobic and larger sized patients. We have the “only true open MRI in Olney!” Other services include CT/CTA, mammography, PET/CT, nuclear medicine, fluoroscopy, bone density measurement, Vertebral Fracture Assessment (VFA), and X-Ray.
</p><p>
	This location also offers the latest technology in obstetrics – 3D ultrasound. Expectant mothers need not visit an ultrasound portrait studio for 3D images of their baby; they will receive free 3D images when they visit us for a physician-prescribed ultrasound exam. Moms can also rest assured that their ultrasound exam is being performed by experienced technologists and expert radiologists who are trained to recognize medically important conditions. Olney Imaging Center also offers a program called SnapIt that allows parents to share their ultrasound photos instantly online with family and friends through Twitter, Facebook, text, email and more.&nbsp;
</p><p>
	Our full-service radiology center is located just 0.2 miles from Medstar Montgomery Medical Center making it convenient location for just about any imaging study your doctor orders. Outpatient radiology services offered at our center are on average half the cost of imaging services performed at a hospital, making it an affordable alternative to hospital imaging without sacrificing quality. &nbsp;
</p><p>
	Community Radiology Associates | Olney Imaging Center serves patients in the communities of Olney, Laytonsville, Brookeville, Burtonsville, Redland, Montgomery Village, Gaithersburg, and more.
</p><p>
	With the advanced knowledge of our expert radiologists, compassionate staff, and state-of-the-art medical imaging technology, we are able to offer you the most exceptional health care service possible. You can count on Olney Imaging Center to provide timely, transparent&nbsp;and accurate information about all aspects of your radiology exam.
