This center provides metro New York’s most-comprehensive suite of diagnostic &amp; oncology imaging services, as well as biopsy &amp; interventional services rivalling or exceeding the technology &amp; expertise of many top hospitals in the region.
</p><p>
	<strong>Driving &amp; Parking</strong>
</p><p>
	LHR 66th Street is located on the upper east side of Manhattan at&nbsp;<a href="https://maps.google.com/maps?saddr=&amp;daddr=400%20E.%2066th%20St.%2C%20New%20York%2C%20NY%2010065">400 East 66<sup>th</sup> Street at 1<sup>st</sup> Avenue</a>, just one block from York Avenue and close to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, NewYork-Presbyterian, Hospital for Special Surgery (HSS) and Weill Cornell Medical Center.
</p><p>
	Our center is near to the Ed Koch Queensboro Bridge to Queens and the FDR Drive and has many <a href="https://goo.gl/maps/NLDDpkfcRamLF4r97">parking garages</a> in close proximity
</p><p>
	<strong>Public Transportation</strong>
</p><p>
