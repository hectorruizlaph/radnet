	Lenox Hill Radiology (LHR) | Cobble Hill is&nbsp;part of LHR’s network of outpatient radiology centers located throughout the New York metropolitan area. Our goal is to provide leading-edge&nbsp;imaging services paired with the most accurate diagnosis and personalized patient care.
</p><p>
	The facility at LHR | Cobble Hill is a beautiful, spacious, and recently expanded full-service medical imaging center.&nbsp;We offer a complete range of imaging services including MRI, CT, 3D Mammography, Ultrasound, DEXA (for bone density measurement), and walk-in digital X-Ray.
</p><p>
	We have the very latest in MRI technology, including two NEW&nbsp;Wide-Open 3T MRI units, which are perfect&nbsp;for claustrophobic and larger-sized patients. The 3T MRI capability offers superior image quality for imaging difficult areas like the brain (neurological), small joints, large joints and muscle (musculoskeletal), and high-resolution prostate imaging.
</p><p>
	As in all of our locations, LHR | Cobble Hill offers 3D Mammography (3D Tomo), the latest technology in breast cancer screening. Studies show that 3D Mammography detects 40% more invasive cancers than traditional mammography alone.* So when it’s time for your annual screening mammogram, trust the 3D Mammography experts at Lenox Hill Radiology.
</p><p>
	LHR | Cobble Hill is located within Downtown Brooklyn, less than 1 mile from The Brooklyn Hospital Center and less than 2 miles from New York-Presbyterian Brooklyn Methodist Hospital. The location is conveniently situated on Smith Street between Warren Street and Baltic Street. If you are traveling by train, take the F or G trains to the Bergen St. station, which is 1 block away. If traveling by bus, the B57 bus stops 1 block away at Smith St./Warren St., and the B65 (Smith St./Bergen St.) bus stops less than 3 blocks away.
